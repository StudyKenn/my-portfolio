About Me

Hi! I'm Kennedy Amucha, a 24-year-old Full Stack Web Developer with a deep love for art and design. Creativity has been a part of my life since childhood, and discovering web development allowed me to channel that passion into a career. With expertise in both Front End and Back End development, I focus on crafting seamless, user-friendly digital experiences. I thrive on problem-solving, attention to detail, and continuously expanding my skill set to stay ahead in this ever-evolving field. To me, web development is an extension of my passion for art. Just as art should be accessible and enjoyable, I believe online experiences should be too. That’s why I’m dedicated to creating functional, intuitive, and inclusive websites—each with a personality to match!
